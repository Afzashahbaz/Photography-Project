Photography Life Reviews

We’ve reviewed hundreds of photography products at Photography Life – cameras, lenses, tripods, bags, post-production software and more. Every time that we review something, our goal is to dive deeper than any other photo review website. Months or years of real-world testing and experience go into everything we write.

Unlike many photography websites, we are not affiliated with any camera equipment company and are never paid by any manufacturer to write a review. All our reviews are unbiased and entirely the author’s own opinion. Although most camera gear nowadays is very good and earns a high rating in our tests, we have also reviewed duds from almost every camera company and always call them out when we do.

If you ever have questions about a product we’ve reviewed or any of our ratings, we will do our best to personally respond to you in the comments of any review. We’re proud to have built a community of photographers across the world who look forward to our reviews and trust us when making their equipment decisions.
